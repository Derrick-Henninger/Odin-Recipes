Green Eggs and Ham Recipe

You thought there was more to green eggs and ham than the title? Think again!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs, green
  • 1 ham, red. Jk, also green!!

Steps

  1. Throw eggs into pan, shell and all and fry until eggs are cooked sunny side up
  2. Place ham in oven and bake for 45min
  3. Serve!
