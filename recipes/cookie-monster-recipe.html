Cookie Monster Cookies Recipe

This recipe will literally turn you into the fricken Cookie Monster! You'll automatically be added to the Sesame Street payroll and enrolled in their 401k and cookie plans. You'll need to get grouch insurance within 30 days of completing the recipe or we send the bird.

Ingredients

  • 10 Cups Flour
  • 20 Cups Sugar
  • 200 tsps Blue
  • A Pinch of Rage
  • 1 Eye of Newt

Steps

  1. In a Large Used Trash Can Add Flour and Sugar
  2. In a Seperate Smaller Garbage Can Add Blue, Rage and Ey
  3. Stir Agressively Until Eye Broken and Ingredients Combined
  4. Let Rest at Room Temperature While Judge Judy Plays on Repeat in the Background for 24hrs
  5. By Now the Eye of Newt Should Have Recombined Into Several Dozen Googley Types of Eyes
  6. Place in Sunlight next to a Cookie Sheet and Watch as The Enraged Dough Walks Itself to the Tray in Golf Ball Size Spheres With Wonky Legs
  7. Cookies Will be Done After 20 Minutes of Exposure to Sunlight and Totally Safe to Eat...
