Worm's Wort Soup Recipe

Are you locked away by an evil scientist that just doesn't doesn't get it? As the Fresh Prince once said, "evil scientists just don't understand!" This recipe can help that ugly little creature go ni-night so you can finally explore the cemetary and warn Jack that his understanding of Christmas town is misinformed.

Ingredients

  • 2 Ribbets of Frog's Breath
  • 3 Cups Worm's Wort
  • A pinch of Deadly Nightshade
  • Add more Frog's Breath if Nightshade gives off an obvious odor

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in witches pot
  2. Stire until hot and serve to unwitting scientist
